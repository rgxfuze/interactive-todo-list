FAQ & Help

Q. Where can i write new tasks?

A. You can write new tasks by typing in the text area.

Q. How to remove tasks?

A. You select / check the task you want to delete and press "Remove Task"

Q. How can i edit tasks?

A. You select / check the task you want to edit and press "Edit Task", A message box will appear that will tell you to input the edited task.

Q. Will my tasks be saved?

A. No. The tasks aren't locally saved.

Q. How to reorder the tasks?

A. You can drag and drop to edit the tasks.
